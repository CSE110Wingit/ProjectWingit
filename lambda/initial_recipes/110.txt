Hot Pina Colada Wings

Chicken wings, pineapple, coconut milk, soy sauce, Sambal Olek, fresh ginger

Try these Hawaiian wings in the Summer in a tiki hut!

1. Put all ingredients for the marinade in a food processor and mix on high speed 3 minutes with the steel blade.
2. Cover the wings with marinade.
3. Marinate the wings for 6 - 12 hours.
4. Grill the wings until the skin gets crispy but not burnt.
5. Enjoy with or without your favorite dip.
