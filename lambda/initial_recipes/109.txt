Buffalo Pumpkin Spice Wings

Frank's RedHot Original Seasoning Blend, brown sugar, McCormick Pumpkin Pie Spice, McCormick Onion Powder, McCormick Ground Mustard, McCormick Garlic Powder, salt, chicken wings, pure maple syrup, vegetable oil

Some chicken wings to enjoy in the fall with coco or chocolate.

1. PREHEAT oven to 450 degrees F. Place wire rack on large shallow foil-lined baking sheet. Spray with no stick cooking spray. Mix RedHot Original Seasoning, brown sugar, spices and salt in small bowl. Place wings in large bowl. Add oil and 1/2 of the seasoning mixture; toss to coat. Arrange wings in single layer on prepared wire rack.
2. BAKE 40 minutes or until wings are cooked through and skin is crispy, turning halfway through cooking.
3. MIX remaining seasoning mixture and maple syrup in large bowl. Add hot cooked wings; toss to coat. Serve immediately.
